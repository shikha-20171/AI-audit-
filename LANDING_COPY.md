## FAQ

### 1. Do I need to sign up before using it?

No. The audit is free and available without login. Email capture only appears after the results are shown.

### 2. Is the savings math AI-generated?

No. The audit logic is rule-based so the pricing recommendations stay deterministic and explainable. AI is only used for the optional personalized summary paragraph.

### 3. Which tools does it support?

The MVP covers Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Gemini, and v0.

### 4. Does it recommend switching tools every time?

No. If the current stack looks disciplined, the app says so. The goal is to surface real overspend, not manufacture fake savings.

### 5. Where does Credex fit in?

If the audit shows meaningful savings potential, especially on recurring AI spend, Credex is positioned as the next step to capture additional upside through discounted credits.
